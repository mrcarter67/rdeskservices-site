Network Management

  • VLAN segmentation — guest, production, OT, management, and DMZ isolation
  • SD-WAN configuration for multi-site government campuses
  • 24/7 NOC monitoring with escalation thresholds and alerting
  • Network documentation and topology diagrams for audits
  • Wireless (WPA3-Enterprise) design and RF site surveys

NAICS 541512

Proactive threat detection, active incident response, and security operations built on Microsoft 365 Defender, Splunk SIEM, and Bitdefender GravityZone. We monitor for BEC campaigns, phishing, APT activity, and insider threats — and respond, not just alert.

  • Splunk SIEM deployment, log ingestion, and alert tuning
  • Bitdefender GravityZone endpoint protection and policy management
  • Business Email Compromise (BEC) identification and containment

  • Incident response playbooks and post-incident documentation
  • FortiGate–Splunk log integration for unified visibility

Cloud Infrastructure
NAICS 541513

Microsoft Azure and M365 architecture, administration, and migration services. We design compliance-grade cloud environments with Zero Trust identity, conditional access policies, and hybrid configurations that meet FedRAMP-aligned security baselines.

  • Microsoft 365 tenant administration and license optimization
  • Azure infrastructure design — VNets, NSGs, ASGs, and policy baselines
  • Entra ID (Azure AD) identity management and MFA enforcement
  • Conditional Access policy design for Zero Trust architecture
  • Exchange Online mail flow, anti-spam, and DMARC/DKIM hardening
  • Cloud migration planning — on-prem to Azure and hybrid environments

Managed Help Desk
NAICS 541519

SLA-backed Tier 1 through Tier 3 support for end users, devices, and infrastructure. We provide both remote and on-site support for government clients, with documented escalation paths, change management processes, and regular reporting for compliance audits.

  • Tier 1–3 support with defined SLA response windows (<2 hour critical)
  • Endpoint management — Windows, macOS, mobile via MDM
  • Patch management with testing, staging, and documented rollout
  • Hardware lifecycle management — procurement, deployment, decommission
  • User onboarding and offboarding (account provisioning/deprovisioning)
  • Monthly service reports and executive summaries

IT Transition Services
NAICS 541611

Structured vendor exit and IT transition management for organizations changing MSPs, contract vehicles, or infrastructure providers. We specialize in zero-downtime cutover planning, SLA breach documentation, and phone/internet number portability coordination.

  • Current-state IT audit and asset inventory (hardware, software, licensing)
  • Vendor contract exit documentation and SLA breach analysis
  • Phone number portability coordination (Comcast Business, AT&T, VoIP)
  • Zero-downtime cutover planning with rollback procedures
  • Executive-ready transition reports for legal and ownership review
  • Knowledge transfer and documentation of inherited environments
